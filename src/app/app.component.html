Colour Pencils Kids Favourite

A coloured pencil is an art medium constructed of a narrow, pigmented core encased in a wooden cylindrical case. Although colored pencils had been used for “checking and marking” for decades prior, it was not until the early 20th century that artist-quality colored pencils were produced.
